🦷 Dental Clinic Analytics Dashboard

End-to-End Healthcare Analytics Project | Excel + Power BI

---

📋 Project Background

This project was built using real operational data from an active dental clinic in Cuttack, Odisha.

After one year of operations, clinic data was stored in Excel with limited visibility into revenue performance, patient behavior, service demand, and geographic trends.

The objective of this project was to transform raw operational records into actionable business insights through data cleaning, validation, analysis, and dashboard development.

Data Period: February 2025 – January 2026

Total Records: 1,384 Patient Visits

Total Revenue Analyzed: ₹8,81,950

---

❓ Business Questions Answered

1. Which treatments generate the highest revenue?
2. How effectively is the clinic retaining patients?
3. What seasonal patterns exist in patient visits and revenue?
4. Which geographic locations contribute the most patients and revenue?
5. How can operational data support better business decisions?

This project involved working with real-world healthcare data rather than a pre-cleaned public dataset.

✅ Data Validation

During analysis, a revenue anomaly was identified within the Consultation category.

Further investigation with clinic stakeholders revealed that a scaling procedure fee had been incorrectly entered under Consultation during manual data entry.

The issue was verified and corrected before final reporting.

This process highlighted the importance of validating business data rather than relying solely on system outputs.

🔍 Key Business Insights

Revenue Concentration

Crown, Bridge, RCT, and Ortho treatments generated the majority of clinic revenue, indicating that advanced procedures were the primary revenue drivers.

Strong Patient Retention

The clinic achieved a 59.4% repeat visit rate, demonstrating strong patient retention during its first year of operations.

Core Patient Segment

The largest patient population belonged to the working-age demographic, highlighting a consistent demand for dental services among adults.

Geographic Reach

While the clinic serves its local area Telengabazaar, significant patient traffic also originated from nearby towns and districts, indicating growing regional visibility and referral-driven growth.

Treatment-Based Revenue Model

Consultations contributed minimal revenue because the clinic offered free consultations during its first year as a patient acquisition strategy.

Most revenue was generated through treatment conversion rather than consultation fees.

🔒 Data Privacy

To protect patient privacy:

- Patient names removed
- Contact numbers removed
- Personally identifiable information excluded
- Analysis performed only on anonymized operational data

The project focuses solely on analytical and business insights derived from non-identifiable data.
